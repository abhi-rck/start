About Energy Club

Energy Club was established in 2007 as a student wing of centre of energy and environment by two of our super seniors. They had participated in Mondialgo Engineering Competition Since then we have been growing continuously. We offer you a platform where you can work on innovative ideas and projects related to our domain "Energy & Environment" Parallel Club Develops necessary skills in an individual like public speaking,team workability,event management skills ,editorial skills are just a few to name.Currently ,the Energy club has 50+ active member who are highly conscious and concerned about the future of energy and sustainability.
